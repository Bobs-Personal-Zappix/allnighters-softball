Team History

The Allnighters · Est. 1992 · Peabody, MA

Our Story

The Allnighters Softball team was founded in 1992 as part of the North Shore Restaurant League in the Peabody, Massachusetts area. In those early days, the team was a coed squad — a mix of players from the restaurant industry who shared a love of the game and a love of a cold one after it.

What started as a fun summer league team quickly developed into something more — a brotherhood (and sisterhood) of players who showed up every season, brought their best, and stuck around long after the final out. Over time, the team transitioned into the Peabody Men's Intramural Softball League (PMISL), where the Allnighters have competed in Division 3 and made their mark on the North Shore softball scene.

Even as the primary league became men's-only, the Allnighters have never forgotten their roots. When charity events, tournaments, or special leagues come around, the doors open back up to coed play — keeping the inclusive spirit of 1992 alive.

Through lineup changes, champion runs, tough seasons, and everything in between, the one constant has been the team itself. The Allnighters represent more than just wins and losses — they represent three-plus decades of community, competition, and camaraderie on the diamond.

Timeline

1992 — The Beginning
The Allnighters are formed as a coed team in the North Shore Restaurant League, Peabody, MA. The name "Allnighters" is chosen — a nod to the late-night restaurant world where many of the original players worked. The iconic logo concept is born: softball meets nightlife.
Mid-1990s — Growing the Roster
The team expands and builds a loyal core group of players. The Allnighters develop a reputation for competitive play and even better post-game hangs. Team traditions begin to take shape.
2000s — Transition to PMISL
The Allnighters transition into the Peabody Men's Intramural Softball League, competing in Division 3. The coed roots are preserved for charity events and special tournaments.
2010s — A Decade of Consistency
Season after season, the Allnighters show up. Roster turnover brings new energy while longtime veterans anchor the lineup. The team continues to grow its legacy in the Peabody softball community.
2023 — New Logo, Same Team
The Allnighters unveil a fresh vintage-style logo — bat, ball, martini glass, beer, crescent moon — honoring the team's founding spirit. New gear follows. The brand is cemented.
2026 — This Season
The Allnighters return for their 34th+ season in the PMISL, armed with a 25-player roster, a new website, and the same hunger that's fueled the team since 1992. The chapter continues.
